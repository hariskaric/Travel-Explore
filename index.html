Gates of the East and the door to the West

It has always been an intersection of important roads from East to the West, from Mediterranean basin to Central Europe and vice versa. Through the history Sarajevo became a bridge where the Orient and the West both clashed and emerged.

Mostar
Old Town

First mentioned in 1452 as Turkish garrison Town, Mostar is chief city, and geographically it is the capital of Herzegovina. Situ ated in mountains country along the Neretva River, Mostar is well-known for Old Bridge which was in 2005 added to UNESCO’s World heritage list.
